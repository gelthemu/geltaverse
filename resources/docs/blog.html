I Call It Geltaverse

By Gel M.

Welcome to Geltaverse — my personal corner of the web. It is not a cryptic place on Mars incidentally. Geltaverse is more than just a website; it’s My Home on the Web.

In addition to this weblog about the things that bring me joy every day, I hope to start writing and sharing stuff as soon as this site goes live. At least I hope so.

Every now and then, I hope to share designs and codes, whether from personal experiments or school projects. Even if things don’t go as planned, I’ll be building valuable skills along the way that will help a lot in other areas.

I hope you find something that resonates with you. You’re welcome!
